# Population Plus One — CapCut Video Storyboard

## Overview
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)
- **Length:** ~30 seconds
- **Platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, App Store Preview
- **Mood:** Emotional, cinematic, powerful
- **Music suggestion:** Soft piano → builds to emotional climax (search CapCut: "emotional cinematic" or "inspiring piano")

---

## How to Use the HTML Animation

1. Open `animation.html` in Chrome (double-click the file)
2. Press **F11** for fullscreen
3. Use **OBS Studio** or **Windows Game Bar** (Win+G) to screen-record
4. The animation auto-plays: 30 seconds, 6 scenes, all timed
5. Import the recording into CapCut

## Enhancing in CapCut

### Adding Real App Screenshots
For scenes 4 and 5, consider overlaying:
- A screen recording of the trading card being created
- The natal chart wheel spinning
- The time capsule scrolling
- Yard sign customization

### Audio Layers
| Layer | Content | Timing |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Music | Emotional piano track | Full 30s, fade in/out |
| SFX 1 | Heartbeat or tick sound | Scene 2 counter |
| SFX 2 | Impact/boom | Scene 3 "+1" appearance |
| SFX 3 | Whoosh | Scene 4 card slides |
| SFX 4 | Subtle dings | Scene 5 badges |

### Text Overlays to Add in CapCut
- Scene 1: Add a subtle glow/shadow to text
- Scene 3: Consider adding "8,153,420,118" (the +1 result) briefly
- Scene 6: Add "Available on the App Store" text

### Color Correction
- Set overall tone to slightly warm
- Boost contrast for the gold elements
- Keep the dark navy deep and rich
